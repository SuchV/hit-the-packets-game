Hit the packets!

Strategy game


Score: 0

Game rules:

1. The game is played on a n x n board (n defined in config).
2. Your goal is to hit all the packets before they fall to the bottom.
3. Yellow windows are your bullets.
4. When you click on them you shoot your bullet and the packets fall.
6. You can go to next action by clicking on the Next action button.
7. If you hit them, you get points and they become red.
8. Game ends when all packets are red or when you run out of ammunition.
